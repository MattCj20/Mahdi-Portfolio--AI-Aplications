### Slide 2 — Mission Challenge
- NASA Earth-observation missions collect large amounts of satellite imagery.
- Tracking deforestation and urban expansion manually is slow and inconsistent.
- The project focuses on automating land-use change detection over time.

### Slide 3 — AI Solution
- Use a change-detection model trained on satellite image pairs.
- Compare images from different dates to identify forest loss and urban growth.
- Output a probability map showing areas of likely change.

### Slide 4 — Testing Plan
- Test predictions against labeled ground-truth satellite images.
- Measure precision, recall, F1 score, and region overlap.
- Validate across different regions to check generalization.

### Slide 5 — Expected Impact
- Faster monitoring of land-use change.
- Better support for NASA Earth science analysis.
- Clearer, scalable way to detect environmental change over time.
